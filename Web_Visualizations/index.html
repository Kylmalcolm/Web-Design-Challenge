Summary: Latitude & Weather

By comparing the environmental data of over 550 cities across the globe it becomes clear that latitude has a significant impact on certain attributes – such as temperature – while not being as significant to others, such as cloudiness. The graphs on this website provide good visualizations that make it easy to see at a glance what impacts latitude does and does not have.

This website provides graphs that compares latitude with temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed, along with some analysis, and also a table of data that informed the data points on the graphs themselves. Further research into the northern and southern hemispheres could be added at a future date to enhance the website. Also addressed here is that some environmental data may be more impacted by the topography of physical features of the cities that make up the data points.
